Art Institute of Chicago

Address

111 South Michigan Avenue
Show Map

The Art Institute of Chicago consistently ranks as one of the world's top art museums and for good reason. The Art Institute has the largest collection of Impressionist paintings outside Europe, and an extraordinary Modern Wing housing nearly 1,000 works from artists the world over.

The upcoming exhibition by Christien Meindertsma "Everything Connects" reveals on March 21 through October 20, 2019. It takes a look at creativity, intelligent design and the role it plays in the production of a variety of products from unprocessed material to items we often take for granted.

    3 out of 5 stars

On Thursday the museum is open late until 8:00 PM and general admission are free of charge.
